Project 6: Ski Lounge Website

Overview

This was my final exam for WEB 1400, intro to web designing. In this project we are supposed to find a topic of our linking, in this case my hobbies, and make a website based off of the topic chosen.

Users

The target in this case was anyone who know me and wants to get to know me better, thus a more personal audience rather than a board audience.

Challenges

Given this was my fist website ever made, the issue was finding the right edits and content for this page. In addition, going outside of my comfort zone to try and make it the best first website ever designed, which in my situation I thought it was perfect!
