Project 4: Pizza App Low Fidelity Prototype

Overview

This is one of my projects made from the google UX certification. The goal is t create throughout the whole course a certain amount of projects, to then finally making your own app that you ended up creating from the start of the course. Mine in this case is a pizza app!

Users

The audience in this project are anyone hungry for some pizza! whenever someone is in the mood for pizza, this app is the way to go, the name is Volpi Pizza Co.

Challenges

The biggest challenge is finding the right design, flow, and architecture for this app. Not everyone will like their favorite app for fast food, and the difficulty of that is to try and find a balance of making the app work, as well as making the architecture adn design cool and attracting!
