Project 1: Pathways Davis Campus Website

Overview

This is one of my first websites created within my weber state job, web designer specialist. Here I helped with the design style, the updating of pdf files and links, and the structure.

Users

The intent of this website is to get users from tech colleges to transfer to weber state university after their programs are almost a completion at their tech colleges.

Challenges

The biggest challenge was the transition from the old website, to the new one. In other words, gathering old data, updating the data, and transferring it to the new one. Lots of files needed editing.
