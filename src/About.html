About Me, Who Am I ?

The Beginning

I was born and raised in Lugano, Switzerland. This is where my journey and passion for UI/UX began. Growing up in Europe opened my eyes to the world. I met many people from many parts of the world, and I realized how we are all different and unique.

Then What ?

After spent my whole life in Europe, I decided that after high school I wanted to find new opportunities and discover what the world has to offer for me and my interest.
That is when I discovered Weber State University, a commuter school in Ogden Utah.

Why Did I choose UX ?

Once I began perusing my degree, I fell in love with it even more. Along the journey I was able to find work as a web designer for the university, freelance, and be involved in some UX research with one of my professors.

Current Situation-ish

I am currently on the search of new opportunities for my next chapter in my career. I expect to graduate Fall, or December, of 2025. My goal is to be bale to be a great asset to any company, and show my skills to better create products and help users to sadify their wants and needs.
