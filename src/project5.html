Project 5: Photoshop Magazine

Overview

This was my final exam for my WEB 2200 class, into to Photoshop. The goal of the final exam was to make a photoshop edit of your desire, and make it as best as possible with the resources learned in class.

Users

The intent of this poster is to attract any cat-lovers out there! This is a fictional cat magazine that is indented to talk about just cats, and only cats! The name of the company is Purrtastic (get it, purr-tastic..lol).

Challenges

The biggest challenge is finding the right gradient of colors. In this case, the magazine is intended for a Christmas edition. Therefore, finding the right colors, the right pictures, and making the right edits were all the most difficult process of this project.
