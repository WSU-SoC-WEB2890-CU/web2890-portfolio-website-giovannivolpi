My Case Study

Mattia Vosti Official Page: Freelance Work 1

Within the world of Web Developing, I wanted to extend my challenges and begin a new chapter in my career, Freelancing. I knew that this would be a big challenge, but it will pay off.

I first began talking to Mattia Vosti, a professional Swiss paraglider who has competing national and international races. He mentioned that he was in need of a website to advertise himself.

This is Where My Freelane Career Begins

I started by creating color sheets, wireframes, and noting down what was necessary to best make this website.

After working on my UX/UI side of the project, I began coding it all out, with a total of 6 pages. The work is still yet to be finished.

What Was the Biggest Challenge You May Think

The biggest challenge was facing this new chapter. It is hard to be able to successfully accomplish a website in just a few days. It is definitely a struggle, but keeping a positive mentality and remembering what your professors taught you is the biggest support you will have in this work environment.
