Project 2: Mattia Vosti Officail Page

Overview

This is one of my first freelance works I had the chance to work on. This is still a work in progress, but it is close to its completion. So far this page has a main page, my story, schedule an appointment to fly, contact me, and my blog.

Users

The scope is to get the attention of anyone in the world of paragliding, and potential sponsors, to support Mattia Vosti. The other side are users who want to fly across the Swiss alps with a guide, which he is licensed to do.

Challenges

The biggest challenge was working with a client 1 on 1. This is a different atmosphere that I am not used to working, and it can get quite difficult depending on what the client wants new, or edited.
